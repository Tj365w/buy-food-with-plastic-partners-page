What types of partnerships are there?

Our work is based on teamwork and good cooperation with other people. You can support as a company or private donor with financial means, or help with donations in kind. We also have many collaborations with companies that are happy to donate a part of their sales to Buy Food with Plastic. We call the concept 'Sell & Do Good'. It is also important for us to exchange and collaborate with organisations worldwide that have the same purpose as we do . Together we can achieve a lot!

Are there donation packets?

Yes, we also have donation packages. We would be happy to present these in a joint meeting and find out together which one suits your company best.

How do I become a partner?

Whether you are a company or an individual, please contact Anna Gracia Herbst directly. She is responsible for partnerships and collaborations at Buy Food with Plastic and can respond to your individual needs.

How can I donate as a company?

We accept one-off and regular donations and are also open to communicative promotion of the partnership.
